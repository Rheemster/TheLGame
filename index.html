The L Game

Move your L. If you can't move, you lose.

F to flip horizontally, R to rotate clockwise, WASD or ARROW KEYS to move, ENTER to confirm.

Under Construction!

What is this?

The L Game is an extremely simple strategic game created by Edward de Bono. It served as the topic for his comprehensive book on strategic thinking "The Five-Day Course in Thinking".

Why did you create this?

The first and foremost point of this project is to learn the basics of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript.

What's next?

As you may have seen already, the other two buttons next to this one are currently useless. Interactive analysis of the current board state and the ability to play against a bot will be implemented next.

How do I tell if I've won?

If you're playing with a friend, the first player unable to move is the loser. You can press Q to play again. If your browser crashes while playing against the AI, you may have won, in which case congratulations! Otherwise, it may be stuck in a corner. Either way, you've outsmarted a computer. You'll need to close and reopen the browser.
